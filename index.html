How Vardaan Works

Quick business registration & Store creation

Hassle-free business registration & store creation is a need for business owners. Now Pick your business type through the default category of the business. Register your business in a few clicks and fill the other business information later. Registered and unregistered businesses can set up businesses and keep accounting active from anywhere in the world. Applicable for GST & non-GST businesses.

Create Store items Instantly

Create your customized store instantly on the phone. The Vardaan app supports small & medium businesses to create customized stores anytime from anywhere. Now no need to maintain any manual ledgers, the flow for the Store item creation is very easy.

Go to Store icon at top right corner to create store item. Provide Itemâ€™s name, Price, Item code(Optional), Categories & GST no. if applicable.

Create Superfast Orders

Create orders with few clicks, add a discount on final estimate of the order. Keep the order live in the order book and edit the order on a special request from the customer. Provide the order summary/estimates/quote online. The order summary/invoice is downloadable and can be shared with customers via Email & chatting apps.

Customer Engagement

All orders belong to a customer, Vardaan application allows you to capture the customer details for future customer engagement through various marketing strategies. We will send automatic SMS to your customers for each order status & You can also send payment alerts, Instant discount offers & any sort of promotional text messages through our SMS service. More intuitive features coming soon..

Business Analytics

Having analytics of your business is a must feature for merchants. Vardaan has inbuilt analytical features that allow merchants to control the business from anywhere and at anytime. Completed order, cancelled order, pending order, number of customers, total business value, monthly business tracker, and many more.
